The Darkest Hour
Operation Sea Lion has succeeded, but the USSR is now ready to fight.
The United States and British Commonwealth have formed a unified command.
This scenario uses the classic rules unless otherwise indicated.
The order of play is altered: Germany, USSR, Japan, USA
Axis wins by controlling 4 capitals.
Allies win by controlling 5 capitals.
The United Kingdom is considered to be a capital, but there are no British
Infantry and Armor defend at 3.
Infantry costs 4. Armor costs 7.
